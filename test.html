Step into a celebration of color, culture, and community at Light the Night 6.0—our biggest event yet!

Light the Night celebrates Diwali, a vibrant holiday cherished by the South Asian community. Known as the Festival of Lights, Diwali is a five-day celebration that symbolizes the triumph of light over darkness and good over evil 🪔 That being said, this year, we will have live performances, a dance floor, photo booths, exciting giveaways, delicious South Asian food, and much more!
